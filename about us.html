About Us

Welcome to our journey of capturing memories that last a lifetime.

Turning moments into memories, one frame at a time.

We are a trio of passionate photographers (Lakshith, Aneesh, and Vamshi) who have been best friends since college. Over the years, we’ve grown from amateur photographers to award-winning professionals, capturing special moments that last a lifetime.

L – Achuta Lakshith Satya Srikar: The Strategist and Event Specialist

Achuta Lakshith Satya Srikar, the “L” in LAV Photography, is the backbone of our team. Known for his vibrant event photography, Lakshith captures the energy and excitement of every moment with a flair that’s unmatched. His exceptional skills have earned him the "Elite Photographer Award" and recognition as the "Best Event Photographer of the Year" in 2023.

Beyond his photography talent, Lakshith plays a vital role as the strategist of LAV Photography. His business acumen ensures seamless project execution and premium services at unbeatable value. From large weddings to corporate events, Lakshith has a knack for transforming moments into timeless visuals.

A – Aneesh: The Visionary and Cinematic Artist

Aneesh, the “A” in LAV Photography, is the visionary behind our creative direction. His unique eye for cinematic storytelling and dramatic compositions has made him an industry leader in fine art photography. Aneesh's approach is all about capturing the raw emotions and essence of each moment.

His work has earned numerous accolades, and his ability to see beauty in every detail has set him apart as one of the most sought-after photographers. Whether it’s capturing the intimate moments of a wedding or producing awe-inspiring visual art, Aneesh brings his distinct style to every frame.

V – Vamshi: The Master of Light and Portrait Photography

Vamshi, the “V” in LAV Photography, is the master of light and portrait photography. Known for his ability to play with light and shadows to create dramatic and emotionally powerful images, Vamshi has built a reputation for capturing stunning portraits that tell a story.

His expertise in both studio and natural lighting has earned him recognition in the industry, and his work is often featured in top photography publications. Vamshi’s approach to portraiture is all about capturing the soul of the subject and creating timeless images that are both artistic and authentic.

Our Journey

LAV Photography isn’t just a studio—it’s a dream turned into reality by three college buddies who shared a passion for capturing life’s beauty. What started as an idea during late-night conversations has grown into an award-winning photography studio that’s making waves.

Together, we’ve received numerous accolades, including the "Golden Frame Award," "Artistic Vision Award," and "Elite Photographer Award." These achievements inspire us to keep pushing boundaries and delivering excellence.

"LAV Photography doesn’t just capture moments—they create memories you’ll cherish forever." – A Happy Client

We’re proud to have worked with some incredible clients, including Bollywood stars like Alia Bhatt, Ranbir Kapoor, Vicky Kaushal, Katrina Kaif, and even the Ambani family. It’s a privilege to be a part of their special moments, and we’re constantly striving to exceed their expectations.

If you’d like to be part of our family and have us capture your precious moments, don’t hesitate to get in touch. We’d love to create memories with you!

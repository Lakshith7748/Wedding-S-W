‟Anand Karaj – The Sacred Union”

Capturing the Anand Karaj is a sacred experience, and my focus is on preserving its serenity and emotional depth. The serene Gurdwara setting, illuminated by divine light, becomes a timeless backdrop in every frame. I captured the bride and groom’s graceful Laavan rounds, reflecting their love and commitment. Candid moments, like parents’ proud smiles and the couple’s quiet reverence, add authenticity to the story. Every vibrant detail—from the bride’s intricate attire to the groom’s regal safa—is highlighted. Wide shots of the congregation in prayer showcase the unity and spirituality of the moment. The soulful kirtan and blessings were photographed to evoke the ceremony’s divine essence. Through my lens, the Anand Karaj becomes a poetic celebration of love, tradition, and faith.
